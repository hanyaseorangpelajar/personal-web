Project

HMIF Logo Classifier

As a computer science student and a member of HMIF, I was interested in developing a machine learning model that can recognize the organization's logo in images. After learning about image classification techniques and using Python and the TensorFlow library, I created a program that can take an image file as input and output a prediction whether it contains the HMIF logo or not.

APIMAS

APIMAS is an on going collaborative web app project designed for college students to share their aspirations, critiques, stories, comments, and more. The project is led by Bhaska, a talented front-end developer, and I, Adri, serve as the backend developer.


As the backend developer, my responsibilities include designing and maintaining the database, creating APIs for Bhaska to consume, and ensuring the overall security and scalability of the platform. I work closely with Bhaska to integrate features and ensure a smooth user experience for our target audience.


Bhaska, on the other hand, is responsible for creating an intuitive and engaging front-end design that captures the essence of our project. He works closely with me to ensure that the design and functionality of the platform align, resulting in a seamless user experience.


Together, we are committed to creating a platform that serves the needs of college students, providing them with a space to share their experiences and connect with others who share similar aspirations. We are excited to bring APIMAS to life and make a positive impact on the college community.
